Support

In case you:

- experience errors of any kind,
- forgot your password,
- want to request a new feature,

please send an email to admin@ugtms.uk.

If you haven't received your login credentials yet by the department staff, you need to visit the official UofG Helpdesk (MyGlasgow > New Helpdesk Request) and create a new request ticket. Staff will match your details and confirm you have been recruited by the university. Afterwards you will receive an email with your credentials.

* Always use your university email when you are requesting support *
