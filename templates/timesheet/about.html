About TMS

Universities often recruit several undergraduate and postgraduate students as lab assistants, tutors, or to assist with marking of student assignments or projects. Following recruitment, lab assistants and/or tutors will normally have to submit monthly timesheets with details of hours worked. These timesheets will have to be signed off by a suitable member of staff before they can receive payments.

Timesheet Management Service's purpose is provide an easy to use web application to automate these processes. All users, from students to staff, should be able to use the service without requiring any tech skills and knowledge.

Students are able to submit worked shifts to their timesheets and keep track of their monthly payrolls.
Verifier staff is able to verify submitted shifts by all students and the Payer is able to pay the reamining verified amount to the students.
